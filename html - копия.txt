<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_TPP_is_passed.2C_and_not_ratified">The TPP is passed, and not ratified</span></h3>
<p>Project Gutenberg is concerned about a new international treaty, the Trans-Pacific Partnership.  The treaty text is online at <a rel="nofollow" class="external free" href="https://ustr.gov/trade-agreements/free-trade-agreements/trans-pacific-partnership/tpp-full-text">https://ustr.gov/trade-agreements/free-trade-agreements/trans-pacific-partnership/tpp-full-text</a> .  It includes a section on "Intellectual Property" which would force copyright term extensions and other restrictions on the public domain.  Visit <a rel="nofollow" class="external free" href="https://www.eff.org/issues/tpp">https://www.eff.org/issues/tpp</a> for some analysis of the TPP's negative impacts.  <i>The TPP has been signed, but not ratified within signatory countries</i>.  There is time to make your views known to your lawmakers.
</p>
